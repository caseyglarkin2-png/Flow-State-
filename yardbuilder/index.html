How It Works

1

Satellite Ingestion

YardBuilder AI accesses high-resolution satellite imagery of your facility through Google Earth API. The system analyzes pixel patterns to identify paved surfaces, building boundaries, and existing infrastructure.

2

Computer Vision Analysis

Deep learning models trained on 10,000+ logistics facilities identify dock doors, loading zones, parking areas, and potential reefer zones. The system recognizes patterns unique to your sector.

3

Generative Configuration

The AI generates an initial yard layout with optimized zones, parking spots, and workflows. You refine it via a drag-and-drop editor—adding reefer zones, customizing spot counts, setting flow rules.

4

Instant Integration

Your Digital Twin is immediately available in Flow State YMS. Gates auto-calibrate to coordinates. Dock sensors integrate. AI-driven moves begin immediately.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the AI mapping?

97% accurate compared to manual surveys. Mismatches are easily corrected in the visual editor.

Can I use older satellite imagery?

Yes. But newer imagery (last 30 days) provides better accuracy. YardBuilder auto-pulls the latest.

What about facilities with shade/shadows?

Our multi-spectral analysis works in various lighting conditions. Overcast, dawn, dusk—all covered.

Do I need GPS equipment pre-installed?

No. YardBuilder works with existing coordinates. GPS integration happens later if desired.

Can the AI learn from my custom zones?

Absolutely. Feedback loops improve the model for your vertical and regional conditions.
