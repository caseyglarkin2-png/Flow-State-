## üéØ Key Strategic Insights

### Product Philosophy
- **Goal**: Keep drivers in truck throughout entire yard process (eliminates 5-15 minutes per trip)
- **Standard Protocol Solution**: Solve 100% of the problem, not 98% with exceptions
- **Workflow Variability**: Different facility types require customized workflows (long vs. short driveways, gate configurations)

### Pricing & ROI Strategy
- **Core Value Prop**: "Pays for itself with paper" (10¬¢/page √ó 2-page BOLs = 20¬¢/shipment)
- **Enhanced vs. Core Model**: 
  - Core: Digital modules (gate automation, dock management)
  - Enhanced: + Gen Logs machine vision (white-labeled)
  - Future: RTLS as separate add-on
- **ROI Justification**: Paper savings (underwritable) + soft benefits (faster turn times, visibility, detention tracking)

### Market Positioning
- **RFQ vs. Consultative Sales**:
  - RFQ (UFP): Best product meeting most requirements at competitive price
  - Consultative: Educate on problem existence and standard protocol solution availability
- **Target Differentiator**: Unlike competitors (Outpost), solution works for diverse inbound traffic, not just closed fleet operations

### Technical Challenges Solved
- **Reefer Automation**: Temperature, set point, fuel level capture (variable display interfaces)
- **Seal Checks**: Photo capture + physical tug verification
- **Edge Cases**: Flip phone users, sensor coverage gaps, driver trustworthiness
- **Headcount Reality**: Some facilities (Lineage) can't eliminate guards, need documentation tools instead
