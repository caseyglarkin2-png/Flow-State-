## Forbidden Phrases (Contradictions to Avoid)

### Overpromises
- ❌ "Guaranteed ROI"
- ❌ "100% dispute elimination"
- ❌ "Double your throughput immediately"
- ❌ "Never miss a deadline again"
- ❌ "Always" / "Never" (absolute claims)

### Off-Brand Positioning
- ❌ "YMS for modern yards" (we are YNS, not YMS)
- ❌ "Better yard management software" (protocol, not software)
- ❌ "AI-powered optimization" (standards-first, AI is downstream)
- ❌ "One-size-fits-all solution" (protocol is standard, implementation adapts)

### Vague Claims
- ❌ "Save time and money" (too generic)
- ❌ "Increase efficiency" (variance reduction is specific)
- ❌ "Streamline operations" (flow through standards is specific)
- ❌ "Cutting-edge technology" (ground source truth is specific)
